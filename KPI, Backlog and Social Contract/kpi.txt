Our three KPI are the following:

Productivity: How many hours each member puts in the project each sprint. Measured in hours per week.
Stress levels: How stressful each sprint has been for the team members in order to redistribute assignments. Mesaured on a scale from 1 to 5.
Team satisfaction: How satisfied is each member with each sprint. Mesaured on a scale from 1 to 5.


Additional KPI:
Motivational levels: How motivated each member feel after each sprint. Mesaured on a scale from 1 to 5.
